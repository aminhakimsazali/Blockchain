ABOUT UM CERTIFICATE VERIFIER

We are developing a Decentralized Application (UM Ceritificate Verifier) on the Ethereum Blockchain

The Undercover team's preposition is to enable verified portability of credentials, starting from certificates to reduce fraudulent use of testamurs or letter of credence to gain clearance access or employment. This issue is widespread in certain countries and counterfeit certificates are unfortunately easily obtainable. Encrypting it before placing it on a blockchain however will minimize risk of reliance on records that could be tampered by black hats, an inside job or an attack vunerability of a SQLi to corrupt the database.
